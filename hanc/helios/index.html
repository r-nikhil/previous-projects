About H.A.N.C

Welcome to India's first negotiatation competition for undergraduates

Hustle, Agree, Negotiate, Confer. Yes, it is what it means, or maybe the other way round. Now, that what we are talking to about. the differenece in perception that would lead to arguments. At some point , so far, you must've been in such a tricky situation. You mustve won the argument simply by your, negotiating, oratory or convincing capabilities and you'd wished someone noticed

Firstly, a launchpad for those of you who believe you have gotten recognition for the skills you exhibit sometimes while in those tricky situations we spoke about. Secondly, certify you for whatever it is you have achieved always paving way for further improvement. Also, to top this we would also do what we do best. Add value to your profile. This isn't any other competition, this is what we here at Pilani, call Project H.A.N.C. So without any further ado, apply, ace, conquer. cheers!
