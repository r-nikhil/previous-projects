About BITS Pilani

The Birla Institute of Technology & Science (BITS), Pilani is a renowned institute of higher education. The primary motive of BITS is to train young men and women to drive innovation, be able and eager to create and put ideas into action.

The institute is a dream come true for our founder Lt. Mr. G.D. Birla - an eminent industrialist, an active participant in the freedom struggle. Over the years, BITS has provided the highest quality technical education to students from all over India admitted on the basis of merit. The quality of the students is reflected in the toughness of the entrance exam (The toughest entrance exam in India).

The mission of the institute is to impart knowledge, educate students in science, technology that will best serve the nation in the 21st century. BITS Pilani prides itself in its ability to mould young men and women into multifaceted responsible citizens.
